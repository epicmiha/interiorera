Storm Damage Repair and Restoration Toronto & GTA

Storm Restoration Experts

Storm Damage in Toronto and the GTA is increasingly becoming one of the largest causes of destruction in the Canada due to intense global weather changes. Our unique position lying just off the Atlantic Ocean exposes us to devastating winds. Although there are precautions to protect yourself in these types of situations your property remains vulnerable to damage which can often remain costly and stressful incidents.

Interior Era are on hand 24/7 to combat any form of storm Damage restoration. With our professional and experienced tradesmen we are able to deal with any eventuality in relation to storm damage repairs effectively and efficiently. Accredited by many of the authorised body’s in the restoration industry Interior Era have become one of the largest Storm Damage repair company’s in the Ontario preforming repairs for some of the most publicised storm incidents in the country.

Our contractors are all Fire and flood qualified to the IICRC highest standards.

Through resorting property’s and dealing with claims within the insurance industry interior have established a fantastic working relationship with one of the Canada’s leading loss adjusters and insurance claims management specialists.

This relationship allows Interior Era to offer a comprehensive storm damage repair service as we would recommend these independent loss adjusters to work in conjunction with our outstanding restoration team to not only assist you in receiving the full settlement amount you are entitled to but to restore you property to its original form.

This type of working relationship within the insurance industry is rare but has been proven time after time that this way of working is beneficial in every aspect of a storm damage insurance claim. The Immediate benefit to this system is you will only ever have to liaise with one company regarding your Storm Damage Claim, weather you are unsure about your claims process or you would simply like to know how the restoration work is coming along you will be designated one claim handler to oversee the entire project.
